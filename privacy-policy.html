Privacy Policy

At EventKick, we take your privacy very seriously. This policy outlines the types of personal information we may collect and how we use it. By using our website, you consent to the terms of this policy.

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account on our website, sign up for our newsletter, or register for an event. We may also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and device type.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information to contact you regarding your account, send you marketing emails, or provide customer support. We may use your non-personal information to improve our website and services, analyze trends, and gather demographic information.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to personalize your experience and analyze how you use our website. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel to track and analyze website usage. These services may use cookies and other tracking technologies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet or other network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Please keep this in mind when submitting personal information to us.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@eventkick.com.
